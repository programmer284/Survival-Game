Tutorial:
Move: W-A-S-D or Arrow Keys
Shoot: Space or Click
Don't touch the red ones. (-1 HP)
Don't get shot by the yellow or blue ones. (-4 HP)
It's harder to dodge blue shooters.
Sometimes you get a shooting rampage.
Upgrade stats to be able to get a higher High Score. You can't upgrade while in-game.
Survive as long as you can.
